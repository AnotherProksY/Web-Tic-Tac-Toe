Tic-Tac-Toe

Instruction:

  1. The player chooses the number of fights and the right of the first move.
  2. In the subsequent bouts of the series, the right of the first move alternates.
  3. The winner is the one whose characters they put on, get the right combination as part of the game.
  4. If there is no triple combination, a draw is obtained!
  5. The field is numbered from 1 to 9, in order to put 'X' or '0', you need to select the corresponding digit.
